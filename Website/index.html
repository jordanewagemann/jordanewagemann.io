About Me

About Me

My name is Jordan and I am from Roanoke, VA. I go by the pronouns she/her/hers. I am currently a junior at James Madison University where I am majoring in Integrated Science and Technology with a concentration in the Environment. After I graduate, I plan to become a Pollution Control Technologist and eventually work for the EPA. Currently I am a Student Manager at Starbucks on my school's campus. I am extremely passionate about service, so when I am not working or in class you can typically find me volunteering within the community. In my free time, I also enjoy painting, hiking, cooking, and reading articles on what is happening in the world around us.

Volunteer Work

Volunteer Work

One of my favorite things to do is give back to others. In high school I volunteered at the SPCA, local libraries, Battered Women's Shelter, etc. I have made an effort to do the same throughout college. In college I have spent much of my time volunteering at the food bank in Verona as well as working at Our Community Place in downtown Harrisonburg Community. Throughout my years of volunteering, I have learned so much about different communities and it has taught me a lot about my own values. Volunteering has been one of the most rewarding things that I have done and I hope to continue with it for a very long time.

Jobs and Extracurriculars

Jobs and Extracurriculars

As I mentioned before hand, I am a student manager at my university's on campus Starbucks. I began working there in Fall of 2019 as a sophomore, and I quickly grew to love it. I have always loved the coffee so the idea of learning more about it and being able to work at Starbucks had alwaus been in the back of my mind, so when I was given the opportunity to do so I ran for it. When it comes to having a job, I take it very seriously because that is how I make a living for the things I need. My determination and leadership skills have allowed me to build meaningful relationships with my coworkers on and off the floor. I also pride myself in recieveing a position like this after only working there for two years. Outside of work, I am also involved in organizations on campus. Some of these organizations include Habitat for Humanity, JMU Walking Club and MYMOM. Each of these have taught me new experiences that I could not have gotten anywhere else. In the Fall of 2021 I am also planning to apply for SafeRides, rush APO, and join Madison Recycling.
